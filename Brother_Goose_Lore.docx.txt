Brother Goose (Brother_Goose_Lore.docx)
Title:
Loyal Lieutenant of the Kurglites
Physical Appearance:
Black and silver power armor
Head of a Canada goose due to warp transformation
Personality:
Unbridled aggression and fury in battle
Extremely loyal to Primarch Kurgle
Key Events:
Transformation during the warp storm on Tharonis IV
The Battle of Drakos Ridge: Goose’s role in leading the counterattack against the Tyranid vanguard, his furious combat style, and how it complemented Luna’s tactics during the battle.
Significant Relationships:
Primarch Kurgle: Sees Kurgle as his anchor, tempering his fury and guiding him.
Luna: Shares a bond with Luna due to her empathetic nature.
